Yunong Liu

MSCS Student at Stanford University

I am a Master's student in Computer Science at Stanford University, advised by Jiajun Wu. I worked on grounding 4D assembly instructions in internet videos.

I worked closely with Weiyu Liu, Cristobal Eyzaguirre, and Manling Li.

I received my BEng in Electronics and Computer Science from the University of Edinburgh, where I am grateful to receive advice from Bonnie Webber. I spent a wonderful semester and summer at the University of Texas at Austin for my exchange program and summer research internship, I worked with Dhiraj Murthy on misinformation detection.
