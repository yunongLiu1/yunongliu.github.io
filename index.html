Yunong Liu

MSCS Student at Stanford University

I am a Master's student in Computer Science at Stanford University, advised by Jiajun Wu.

I am working closely with Weiyu Liu, Cristobal Eyzaguirre, Manling Li and Juan Carlos Niebles on grounding 4D assembly instructions in internet videos.

I received my BEng in Electronics and Computer Science from the University of Edinburgh, where I ranked 2nd in the first 2 years. During my time there, I was fortunate to work with Bonnie Webber and Xixian Liao on natural language processing research. Professor Webber is a role model for me, inspiring me with her unwavering passion and dedication to the field.

I also spent a wonderful semester and summer at the University of Texas at Austin for my exchange program and summer research internship, where I worked with Dhiraj Murthy on misinformation detection.
